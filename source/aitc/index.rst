=========================
Apps-In-The-Cloud Service
=========================


Goal of the Service
===================

The Apps-In-The-Cloud service provides storage for the authoritative list of
a user's Apps and Devices in the Open Web Apps [1]_ ecosystem.

.. [1] https://www.mozilla.org/en-US/apps/
